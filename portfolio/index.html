About Me

I am a student photographer with a deep passion for capturing raw emotions and the beauty of authentic moments. Specializing in **concert and low-light photography**, I thrive in dynamic environments where I can preserve the energy, mood, and intensity of each scene. My work focuses on blending aesthetics with storytelling, ensuring that every frame resonates with emotion and depth.

Beyond concerts, I am also open to **creative and commercial shoot inquiries**, where I aim to bring unique artistic perspectives to each project. Whether it’s the electric atmosphere of a live performance or the subtle elegance of a carefully curated shoot, I am dedicated to crafting visually compelling images that leave a lasting impact.
